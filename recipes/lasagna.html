Description: Vegetable Lasagna

A warm and hearty vegetable lasagna that even the pickiest eaters will succumb to, because lasagna.

Healthy, nutritious, and flavorful lasagna that will fill you up throughout the four seasons. The ingredients are quite flexible and versatile because you can omit whatever you don't like & add whatever vegetables/seasoning you prefer. Just remember to keep the basics: marinara sauce, ricotta cheese, eggs, and lasagna flat noodles

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta noodles
  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • ¾ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¾ cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars pasta sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 (15 ounce) container part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
